About Me

Hi, I'm Sivamani Vinukonda. I've always been driven by a core question in our digital world: "In a sea of data, how do we find the attacker and stop the threat?" This question drove me directly into the world of cybersecurity, not just as a career, but as a personal mission to become a skilled defender in a Security Operations Center (SOC).

I am transforming this curiosity into practical skills, especially in threat detection and response. My journey is hands-on, focused on mastering SIEM tools, and I have experience using Splunk to analyze logs and hunt for security events. My Top 8% global rank on TryHackMe isn't just a number; it's proof of my problem-solving mindset, which I am actively sharpening by advancing through the 'Security Analyst' pathway to master SIEM, threat intelligence, and incident response.

My defensive skills are built on a strong foundation of modern infrastructure. I use Python and Terraform to automate secure cloud environments on AWS and Azure , and I understand the principles of vulnerability management and network security. I love digging into complex issues, understanding how systems are compromised, and figuring out how to defend them.
